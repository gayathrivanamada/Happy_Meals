Malai Kofta

For the Koftas (Dumplings):

  1. 2 cups boiled and mashed potatoes
  2. 1/2 cup crumbled paneer (Indian cottage cheese)
  3. 1/4 cup grated carrots
  4. 1/4 cup green peas (boiled)
  5. 2 tablespoons cornflour or all-purpose flour
  6. 2 tablespoons chopped cilantro (coriander leaves)
  7. 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  8. 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  9. Salt to taste
  10. Oil for frying

    11. For the Gravy:

  11. 1 cup chopped onions
  12. 1/2 cup chopped tomatoes
  13. 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  14. 1/4 cup cashews (soaked in warm water)
  15. 1/4 cup heavy cream
  16. 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  17. 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  18. 1 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  19. teaspoon ground coriander
  20. 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  21. 1/2 teaspoon dried fenugreek leaves (kasuri methi)
  22. 2 tablespoons oil or ghee
  23. Salt to taste
