Palak Paneer

    For the Palak (Spinach) Puree:
  1. Spinach (Palak): 2 bunches or approximately 500 grams
  2. Water: 4 cups (for blanching)
  3. Paneer (Indian cottage cheese): 200 grams, cut into cubes
  4. Cooking oil or ghee (clarified butter): 2 tablespoons
  5. Cumin seeds: 1 teaspoon
  6. Onion: 1 medium-sized, finely chopped
  7. Garlic: 4-5 cloves, minced
  8. Ginger: 1-inch piece, grated or minced
  9. Green chilies: 2-3, finely chopped (adjust according to your spice preference)
  10. Tomatoes: 2 medium-sized, finely chopped or pureed
  11. Turmeric powder: 1/2 teaspoon
  12. Red chili powder: 1/2 teaspoon (adjust to taste)
  13. Coriander powder: 1 teaspoon
  14. Garam masala: 1/2 teaspoon
  15. Salt: To taste
  16. Fresh cream: 2 tablespoons (optional, for garnishing)
